The Orrery

Inspired by the 18th-19th century mechanical orreries, this digital construction displays the motion of the planets around the Sun. Explore the movements and learn about the science behind their orbits.

The Science Behind the Orrery

The orbital periods of the planets are scaled for simplicity. Mercury orbits in 1 second, Venus in 3 seconds, and Earth in 5 seconds. The sizes and distances are not to scale, as it's challenging to represent them accurately on a screen.
